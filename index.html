What is Fusari Pro

At Fusari Pro, we are a supplier specialize in OEM & white label garment production, offering high-quality, tailor-made products

We are a French-Italian company proudly based in Shanghai, with also have offices in Marseille, France and Torino, Italy.

We manage an extensive network of over 20 top manufacturers, each excelling in 10 areas of expertise for A TOTAL LOOKS SUPPLIER: Suiting, Casual-wear, Outwear, Cashmere sweaters, Shirting, Jeans & Chinos, Sneakers, Leather shoes, Leathers bag, Accesories

For the past 15 years, we have dedicated ourselves to perfecting production details, enhancing supply chain efficiency, and driving products innovation, simplifying the order process. Our commitment to quality and precision ensures that we deliver exceptional garments tailored to your needs.
