Our Retail Operation Model

Made to Order: Our service offers an ideal blend of personalization and efficiency, delivering your tailored garment right to your door in just 2-3 weeks. You can begin with standard size samples, make fit adjustments to suit your preferences, or go straight from measurements to fully customize your piece. Perfect for those who value both speed and style, this option makes up 60% of our offerings, ensuring a seamless experience from selection to delivery.

Made to Measure: For those seeking the ultimate in craftsmanship and individuality, our Made to Measure service provides a truly bespoke experience. Available by appointment, this option is perfect for suit orders or VIP clients, with a delivery time of 3-4 weeks. Representing 20% of our services, this is the top choice for those who cherish the art of personalized fashion.

Ready to Wear Collection: Ideal for those in search of timeless, versatile pieces, our Ready to Wear collection includes bestsellers and popular gifts. This category accounts for 20% of our offerings and features items that are ready to ship, perfect for customers who desire effortless style without the wait.
