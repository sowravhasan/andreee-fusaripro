Privacy Policy

At Atelier Fusari ("we", "us", or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website [atelier-fusari.com] and use our services. By accessing and using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. This information includes:

Personal Information:
  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Payment information (e.g., credit card details, billing address)
  • Account details (e.g., username, password)
Usage Data:
  • IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and other usage statistics
Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience, personalize content, and analyze website traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Process Orders: To process and complete your purchases, including billing and shipping.
  • To Improve Our Services: To improve the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • To Communicate with You: To send promotional emails, newsletters, and updates about our products and services (with your consent).
  • For Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.

3. Legal Basis for Processing Your Information (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we rely on the following legal bases to process your personal data:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our contractual obligations with you.
  • Consent: Based on your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our website or prevent fraud, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with legal obligations such as tax or accounting requirements.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with the following parties:

  • Service Providers: For processing orders, payments, shipping, or analytics.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, such as a subpoena or court order.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and tailor content to your preferences. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our site.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. After this period, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including encryption and secure payment processing. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access
  • Right to Rectification
  • Right to Erasure
  • Right to Restriction
  • Right to Object
  • Right to Data Portability
  • Right to Withdraw Consent
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint

To exercise these rights, please contact us at marketing@atelier-fusari.com.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside of China, your data may be processed in China or other countries. We use safeguards such as standard contractual clauses to ensure GDPR-compliant protection.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have, please contact us to have it removed.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version and update the "Effective Date" on the page. Please check this page periodically for changes.
